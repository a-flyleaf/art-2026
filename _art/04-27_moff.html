Caught a Venomoth in a recent Pokémon FireRed playthrough and it was surprisingly good! That and its big ol' eyes nudged it up my Bug-type favoritism rankings some. This is a very simple model with an even simpler rig. It's based on [a specific plush](https://www.pokemoncenter.com/product/701-03830/venomoth-sitting-cuties-plush-6-in), and although I wasn't counting polys, it happens to clock in at 1,500 tris exactly. Also, both wings flap with one (1) driver-controlled bone movement. Neat! I'm also pretty happy with how the colors turned out! The entire base palette uses [named HTML colors](https://htmlcolorcodes.com/color-names/), just to keep it simple, but the real magic is in the shader nodes. [Voilà:](https://old.reddit.com/r/blender/comments/1eqlwuw/no_idea_how_to_apply_hue_shifting_by_light_level/) Realism be darned, I like colors. And doofy eyes.
