# 6/21: hipster 'yote
	Recently trawled through my [Toyshelf]() again, got reminded of this fella---particularly his [one-off Halloween plush](). Sometimes one has to draw their >decade-old self-insert as an anthro, as a treat.
	
	[WIPs]
	<CAPTION>Animal head and legs on a human(oid) torso, no problem. Arms and hands, meanwhile... *eh.* Slade's peculiar underside markings didn't really help. He also looked barefoot with full detail and bare feet, so *bam*, blocky loafers be upon ye.
	Shirt lines were automatic via FireAlpaca's built-in parallel line tool; didn't feel like drawing all that around the body proper. Pocket placement is also a little odd since, if you squint, he was originally supposed to be holding some kind of satchel but I scrapped that in lining. *Oh well* little self-crits to keep in mind next time...!!</CAPTION>
	
	I don't really know how glasses or shoes work with furry anatomy, but the former took a cue from [this reddit post](https://old.reddit.com/r/furry/comments/10v0sbw/you_know_normal_glasses_just_wouldnt_work_on_most/j7g5erl/). Extremely nerdy, which isn't not fitting.
	
	TIME TAKEN: ~3 hours. Not bad for a fullbody! Would've taken a lot longer if I added fur detail or cel shading, but I thought it looked nice enough semi-flat like this.
